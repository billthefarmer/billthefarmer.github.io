Old Shoreham Buccaneers

We are a recently formed morris side based in Shoreham-by-Sea, West Sussex. We practise in The Shoreham Centre on Tuesday evenings during the winter, and dance out from May to September in the summer. 2011 was our first season dancing out.

Join us

Come and join us. If you are interested come and talk to us at one of our performances, or contact us on oldshorehambuccaneers@yahoo.co.uk. We will be pleased to hear from you.

Book us

If you are interested in booking us to perform at your event, party or wedding, etc, please contact us on oldshorehambuccaneers@yahoo.co.uk. We will try our best to help if we can.

Logo

Our logo is based on the boat from the 13th century borough seal of New Shoreham, which is on the Adur and Worthing web site.
